TunaiKita

TunaiKita is Indonesia’s first technology company to develop and deploy a ‘lending robot’ that uses the data you provide through our mobile app on your smartphone and online accounts. Our lending robot evaluates your user profile and assigns you a credit score with which you can apply for cash loans. We currently offer cash installment loans of Rp.4 to 20 million that you can repay over tenures of 3 to 6 months. We have also launched short-term loans of Rp.1 to 5 million with flexible 10 to 30 days tenure.
