Management Team

Board of Commissioners

Simon Halim

Commissioner

Mr. Simon Halim is a Partner/Managing Partner at Arghajata Consulting, a consulting firm. Mr. Halim has more than 26 years of financial and management consultant experience. Mr. Halim started his career in 1984 as an accountant and his last position was as Executive Partner in an accounting firm, KPMG Hanadi Sudjendro & Rekan in 1995. From 1995 to 1998, he held several senior positions in several companies as a commissioner and director in the field of telecommunication, multifinance and coal mining. From 1999 to 2005, he was a partner at Ernst & Young, Hanadi Sarwoko & Sandjaja and served as its Chief Executive Officer. He has been Independent Commissioner of PT Jasa Angkasa Semesta Tbk since June 2010. He served as Independent Commissioner at PT Mitra Investindo Tbk. He served as Independent Commissioner of Apexindo Pratama Duta tbk PT. He holds a Bachelor Degree in Accounting from Universitas Katholik Parahyangan, Bandung and a Registered Public Accountant.

Andi S. Boediman

Commissioner

Andi sits as a Commissioner in Bhinneka.com, the leading B2B e-commerce in Indonesia. He is actively investing in Indonesia film industry ecosystem through Ideosource Film Fund. And he founded IDS | International Design School, a visual communication school in Design, Animation & Filmmaking.

Ipung Kurnia

Chairman

Mr. Ipung Kurnia has been the President Commissioner of PT Hero Supermarket Tbk since December 2008 and its Commissioner since June 2008. Mr. Kurnia served as President Director of PT Hero Supermarket Tbk from 1992 to 2008 and its Director from 1989 to 1992. He is a member of the Honorary Committee of APRINDO (Indonesian Retail and Merchants Association). He received a Bachelor Degree in Marketing from the Simon Fraser University, Canada in 1986 and holds an MBA Degree in Business and Finance from the City University, Canada. He also attended the Advanced Management Program at the Harvard Business School, Boston, USA in 1999.

Board of Directors

Dian Kurniadi

Chief Operating Officer

Mr. Dian Kurniadi have over 23 years of experience in technology entrepreneurship. Currently, he is as partner in Jas Kapital and CTO in Digital Artha Media (Mandiri e-cash) while he is also active as head as E-Payment working group at Indonesia Fintech Association (AFTECH). Mr. Kurniadi started his career in 1995 as Avionics Engineer and starting his first digital startup with his partners in 1996. From 1996 to 2010 he held several senior positions and advisors in several companies ranging from consulting, retail, telecommunication, banking and financial technology. He holds a Bachelor Degree in Electrical Engineering from Universitas Trisakti, Jakarta.

Izak Jenie

Chief Executive Officer
